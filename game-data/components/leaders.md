## Hannibal {#leader-hannibal}

- Increases Strength of all three Punic Wars.
- May combine with Hamilcar.
- If matched with 2nd Punic War roll 1d6 at the end of every Forum Phase to destroy a Tax Farmer until defeated.

## Hamilcar {#leader-hamilcar}

- Increases Strength of all three Punic Wars.
- May combine with Hannibal.

## Philip V {#leader-philip}

- Increases Strength of all four Macedonian Wars.

## Antiochus III {#leader-antiochus}

- Increases Strength of Syrian War.

## Viriathus {#leader-viriathus}

- Increases Strength of all Spanish Revolts IF the required Province(s) for the Revolt(s) exist.
- WIll not automatically activate Inactive Spanish Revolts until the required Province(s) for the Revolt(s) are created; until that time he resides in the Curia and makes aging rolls.

## Mithridates VI {#leader-mithridates}

- Increases Strength of all four Mithridatic Wars.

## Spartacus {#leader-spartacus}

- Increases Strength of all three Slave Revolts.
- If matched with Gladiator Revolt roll 1d6 at the end of every Forum Phase to destroy a Tax Farmer until defeated.

## Vercingetorix {#leader-vercingetorix}

- Increases Strength of all three Gallic Wars.

## Cleopatra VII {#leader-cleopatra}

- KEEP IN HAND WHEN DRAWN.
- Play before Alexandrine War resolution for an effect of the player's choice.
  - EITHER: Give the Roman Commander +3 Military, 50T, but −5 Popularity.
  - OR: Play as a +3 Enemy Leader matching the Alexandrine War.
- Either +3 effect remains until the Alexandrine War leaves play.
