## Africa {#province-africa}

- Created by: 3rd Punic War
- Defends: Jugurthine War

## Asia {#province-asia}

- Created by: Pergamon Bequest
- Defends: all Mithridatic Wars, Parthian War and Syrian War

## Bithynia {#province-bithynia}

- Created by: Bithynian Bequest
- Defends: all Mithridatic Wars, Parthian War, Syrian War and 1st Cilician Pirates

## Cilicia et Cyprus {#province-cilicia-et-cyprus}

- Created by: 1st Cilician Pirates
- Defends: Parthian War and 2nd Cilician Pirates

## Creta et Cyrenaica {#province-creta-et-cyrenaica}

- Frontier Province
- Created by: Bequest or 2nd Cilician Pirates
- Defends: 2nd Cilician Pirates

## Gallia Cisalpina {#province-gallia-cisalpina}

- Frontier Province
- Created by: 1st Gallic War
- Defends: all Gallic Wars, Germanic Migrations and 2nd Punic War

## Gallia Narbonensis {#province-gallia-narbonensis}

- Created by: Germanic Migration
- Defends: all Gallic Wars and 2nd Punic War

## Gallia Transalpina {#province-gallia-transalpina}

- Frontier Province
- Created by: 2nd/3rd Gallic War
- Defends: all Gallic Wars and Germanic Migrations

## Hispania Citerior {#province-hispania-citerior}

- Frontier Province
- Created by: 2nd Punic War
- Defends: All Spanish Revolts

## Hispania Ulterior {#province-hispania-ulterior}

- Frontier Province
- Created by: 2nd Punic War
- Defends: 1st and 3rd Spanish Revolts

## Illyricum {#province-illyricum}

- Frontier Province
- Created by: both Illyrian Wars
- Defends: all Macedonian Wars and Germanic Migrations

## Macedonia {#province-macedonia}

- Frontier Province
- Created by: 4th Macedonian War
- Defends: all Macedonian Wars, Illyrian War, Syrian War and 1st Mithridatic War

## Sardinia et Corsica {#province-sardinia-et-corsica}

- Created by: 1st Punic War
- Defends: 1st Cilician Pirates

## Sicilia {#province-sicilia}

- Created by: 1st Punic War
- Defends: 1st and 2nd Slave Revolts

## Syria {#province-syria}

- Created by: 3rd Mithridatic War
- Defends: Parthian War and all Mithridatic Wars
