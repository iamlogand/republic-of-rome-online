## 1st Gallic War {#war-1st-gallic}

- Immediately active.
- Victory creates Province of Gallia Cisalpina.
- Attacks: Gallia Cisalpina, Gallia Narbonensis and Gallia Transalpina.

## 1st Illyrian War {#war-1st-illyrian}

- Inactive until attacked or matched.
- Creates or worsens Drought conditions even if inactive.
- Creates Province of Illyricum if BOTH Illyrian Wars are defeated.
- Attacks: Macedonia.

## 2nd Illyrian War {#war-2nd-illyrian}

- Immediately active.
- Creates Province of Illyricum if BOTH Illyrian Wars are defeated.
- Attacks: Macedonia.

## 1st Macedonian War {#war-1st-macedonian}

- Immediately active.
- Revolt if Macedonian is a Province.
- Attacks: Illyricum and Macedonia.

## 2nd Macedonian War {#war-2nd-macedonian}

- Inactive until attacked or matched.
- Revolt if Macedonian is a Province.
- Attacks: Illyricum and Macedonia.

## 1st Punic War {#war-1st-punic}

- Inactive until attacked or matched.
- Victory creates Provinces of Sicilia and Sardinia.

## 2nd Punic War {#war-2nd-punic}

- Immediately active.
- If active roll 1d6 at the end of every Forum Phase to destroy a Tax Farmer until defeated.
- Victory creates Provinces of Hispania Citerior and Hispania Ulterior.
- Attacks: Gallia Cisalpina and Gallia Narbonensis.

## Syrian War {#war-syrian}

- Immediately active.
- Revolt if Syria is a Province.
- Attacks: Bithynia, Macedonia and Asia.

## (1st) Cilician Pirates {#war-1st-cilician-pirates}

- Inactive until attacked or matched.
- Creates or worsens Drought conditions even if inactive.
- Victory creates Province of Cilicia et Cyprus.
- Attacks: Corsica et Sardinia and Bithynia.

## Germanic Migrations {#war-germanic-migrations}

- Immediately active.
- Immediately creates Province of Gallia Narbonensis once attacked regardless of battle result.
- Attacks: Gallia Cisalpina, Illyricum and Gallia Transalpina.

## Jugurthine War {#war-jugurthine}

- Immediately active.
- Attacks: Africa.

## Lusitanian War {#war-spanish-revolts}

- If Hispania Citerior and Hispania Ulterior are not yet Provinces, treat as Inactive until the Revolution Phase that they are.
- Attacks: Hispania Citerior and Hispania Ulterior.

## 3rd Macedonian War {#war-3rd-macedonian}

- Immediately active.
- Revolt if Macedonia is a Province.
- Attacks: Illyricum and Macedonia.

## 4th Macedonian War {#war-4th-macedonian}

- Immediately active.
- Victory creates Province of Macedonia.
- Attacks: Macedonia.

## Numantine War {#war-numantine}

- If Hispania Citerior and Hispania Ulterior are not yet Provinces, treat as Inactive until the Revolution Phase that they are.
- Attacks: Hispania Citerior.

## 3rd Punic War {#war-3rd-punic}

- Immediately active.
- Victory creates Province of Africa.

## (1st) Sicilian Slave Revolt {#war-1st-sicilian-slave}

- Eliminates Sicilian Grain Concession.
- If Sicilia is not yet a Province, treat as Inactive until the Revolution Phase in which it is.
- Creates or worsens Drought conditions even if inactive.
- Attacks: Sicilia.

## (2nd) Sicilian Slave Revolt {#war-2nd-sicilian-slave}

- Eliminates Sicilian Grain Concession.
- If Sicilia is not yet a Province, treat as Inactive until the Revolution Phase in which it is.
- Creates or worsens Drought conditions even if inactive.
- Attacks: Sicilia.

## Alexandrine War {#war-alexandrine}

- Immediately active.
- Eliminates Aegyptian Grain Concession.
- Creates or worsens Drought conditions even if inactive.

## (2nd) Cilician Pirates {#war-2nd-cilician-pirates}

- Inactive until attacked or matched.
- Creates or worsens Drought conditions even if inactive.
- Attacks: Cilicia et Cyprus and Creta et Cyrenaica.

## 2nd Gallic War {#war-2nd-gallic}

- Inactive until attacked or matched.
- Revolt if the Province of Gallia Transalpina exists.
- Victory creates the Province of Gallia Transalpina.
- Attacks: Gallia Cisalpina, Gallia Narbonensis and Gallia Transalpina.

## 3rd Gallic War {#war-3rd-gallic}

- Immediately active.
- Revolt if the Province of Gallia Transalpina exists.
- Victory creates the Province of Gallia Transalpina.
- Attacks: Gallia Cisalpina, Gallia Narbonensis and Gallia Transalpina.

## Gladiator Revolt {#war-gladiator}

- Immediately active.
- If active roll 1d6 at the end of every Forum Phase to destroy a Tax Farmer until defeated.

## Invasion of Britannia {#war-invasion-Britannia}

- Inactive until Rome attacks.

## Invasion of Germania {#war-invasion-Germania}

- Inactive until Rome attacks.

## 1st Mithridatic War {#war-1st-mithridatic}

- Immediately active.
- Revolt if Bithynia is a Province.
- Attacks: Bithynia, Syria, Macedonia and Asia.

## 2nd Mithridatic War {#war-2nd-mithridatic}

- Inactive until attacked or matched.
- Revolt if Bithynia is a Province.
- Attacks: Bithynia, Syria and Asia.

## 3rd Mithridatic War {#war-3rd-mithridatic}

- Immediately active.
- Victory creates Province of Syria and the Developed Province of Bithynia (Bithynia-Pontus).
- If the Province of Bithynia exists but is undeveloped victory automatically develops the Province. No Influence is awarded for development in this way.
- Attacks: Syria and Asia.

## Parthian War {#war-parthian}

- Immediately active.
- Attacks: Cilicia et Cyprus, Bithynia, Syria and Asia.

## Sertorian Revolt {#war-sertorian}

- If Hispania Citerior and Hispania Ulterior are not yet Provinces, treat as Inactive until the Revolution Phase in which they are.
- Attacks: Hispania Citerior and Hispania Ulterior.

## Social War {#war-social}

- Immediately active.
- Activates all Inactive Wars and Revolts that are part of a Matching War Set.
- Does not increase the Strength of any Matching Wars.
