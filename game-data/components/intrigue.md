## Assassin {#intrigue-assassin}

- Support for an assassination plot is widespread.
- Add 1 to your Assassination attempt and any Bodyguard rerolls. Play before resolution and discard after use.
- Cumulative effect with other Bodyguard and Assassin cards.

| Modified 1d6 | Result    |
| ------------ | --------- |
| ≥ 5          | Killed    |
| 3–4          | No effect |
| ≤ 2          | Caught    |

## Blackmail {#intrigue-blackmail}

- For your Persuasion Attempt on your initiative, make an unopposed Persuasion Attempt against a non-Faction Leader Senator of your choice.
- No Talents may be spend to defend against your only Persuasion Attempt this Initiative. Talents already on te target still count.
- If it fails, the target Senator's Influence and Popularity are each reduced by 2d6.

## Graft {#intrigue-graft}

- A Senator in Rome may cancel a Tribune veto or proposal by paying 1d6T (from Personal Treasury) to the Bank.
- This does not stop another tribune from being played for the same purpose.
- The Graft may be cancelled if the rolled amount is too high but the card is still discarded. If the targeted Tribune is used to make a proposal, this card must be played before any votes are called.

## Proscription {#intrigue-proscription}

- Playable during the Combat Phase immediately after a victory (by either side) in a Civil War involving the Senate and a Rebel.
- The HRAO may eliminate all the Knights on the board and receive 1d6T per Knight into his personal treasury.
- Henceforth, his Faction may not recruit Knights and all other players may add 1 to their Knight Attraction roll.
- If the HRAO declines, this card is discarded and has no effect.
- For the purpose of determining the HRAO for this card all Victorious Commanders are considered to be in Rome.

## Mob Incited to Violence {#intrigue-mob-incited-to-violence}

- This card may be played against any player who just resolved (whether successful or not) a Murder of a Tribune or Graft Intrigue Card.
- Draw 1d6 Mortality chits plus the Oratory rating of one of your Senators in Rome.
- Chits corresponding to Senators in Rome belonging to the Faction of the player cancelling the Tribune are killed.

## Influence Peddling {#intrigue-influence-peddling}

- You may discard this card anytime other than during the Revolution Phase and draw an unplayed card at random from an opponent of your choice.

## Secret Bodyguard {#intrigue-secret-bodyguard}

- Playable after any Assassination attempt resolution on one of your Senators to reduce the result by 1. Discard after use.
- If the Assassin is not caught then, regardless of the original result, an additional roll must be made per Secret Bodyguard played just to try and catch the Assassin. The re-rolls use the original roll modifier and any "Killed" result is ignored.

## Open Bodyguard {#intrigue-open-bodyguard}

- May be played only during the Revenue Phase on a Senator in Rome belonging to the card player. That Senator must now pay 1T and one Popularity per turn during the Revenue Phase. It remains in effect with that Senator until he dies or fails to make payment (at which time it is discarded). This senator may subtract 2 from all Assassin attempts on him.
- If the Assassin wasn't caught then, regardless of the original result, an additional roll must be made per Bodyguard card played just to try and catch the Assassin. These re-rolls use the original roll modifier and any "Killed" result is ignored.

## Murder of a Tribune {#intrigue-murder-of-a-tribune}

- One of your Senators in Rome may attempt to cancel the use of any one Tribune by rolling an even result on a 1d6. Regardless of the result, that Senator is under suspicion and loses Popularity equal to the die roll. This does not stop another Tribune from being played for the same purpose.
- If the targeted Tribune is to make a proposal the attempt must be made before any votes are called.

## Seduction {#intrigue-seduction}

- For your Persuasion Attempt on your initiative, make an unopposed Persuasion Attempt against a non-Faction Leader Senator of your choice.
- No Talents may be spent to defend against your only Persuasion Attempt this Initiative. Talents already on the target still count.
- Playable immediately before announcing your target.

## Tribune {#intrigue-tribune}

- May be used to bypass Presiding Magistrate to initiate proposal.
- May veto a Prosecution or proposal prior to or during a Faction's turn to vote.
- May not veto last eligible candidate, Consul for Life, Dictator Proposals or Assassination Prosecutions.
