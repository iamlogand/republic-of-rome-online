## 1A — P. Cornelius Scipio Africanus {#statesman-1a}

- Nullifies Disaster / Standoff numbers on Punic Wars but not on any Leader(s).

## 2A — Q. Fabius Maximus Verrucosus Cunctator {#statesman-2a}

- Halve all losses (rounded up) in Combat unless Master of Horse.

## 18A — T. Quinctius Flamininus {#statesman-18a}

- Nullifies Disaster / Standoff numbers on Macedonian Wars but not on any Leader(s).

## 19A — L. Aemilius Paullus Macedonicus {#statesman-19a}

- Nullifies Disaster / Standoff numbers on Macedonian Wars but not on any Leader(s).

## 22A — M. Porcius Cato the Elder {#statesman-22a}

- 1 Free Tribune per turn.
- +6 votes on Law Proposals.

## 1B — P. Cornelius Scipio Aemilianus Africanus {#statesman-1b}

- Nullifies Disaster / Standoff on 3rd Punic war and Spanish Revolts but not any Leader(s).

## 1C — L. Cornelius Sulla {#statesman-1c}

- Nullifies Disaster / Standoff on Social and Mithridatic Wars but not any Leader(s).

## 7A — M. Fulvius Flaccus {#statesman-7a}

- May claim unheld Land Commissioner Concession during Senate Phase any time after Prosecutions provided a Land Bill is in effect.

## 21A — C. Servilius Glaucia {#statesman-21a}

- Mob Orator: May roll 3d6 when making a Popular Appeal.

## 23A — P. Popillius Laenas {#statesman-23a}

- May veto one Land Bill / Turn without using a Tribune card.

## 25A — T. Sempronius Gracchus {#statesman-25a}

- May propose 1 Land Bill / turn without the use of a Tribune card.
- May be played even if 25B is already in play.

## 25B — C. Sempronius Gracchus {#statesman-25b}

- May propose 1 Land Bill / turn without the use of a Tribune card.
- May be played even if 25A is already in play.

## 27A — C. Marius {#statesman-27a}

- +1 to Knight roll.
- Nullifies Disaster / Standoff on Social and Jugurthine Wars but not any Leader(s).

## 4A — C. Julius Caesar {#statesman-4a}

- May convert 2 legions to allies veteran legions (instead of the normal 1) for each victory.

## 22B — M. Porcius Cato the Younger {#statesman-22b}

- 1 Free Tribune per turn.
- +6 votes and double Knights on Law Proposals.

## 28A — M. Tullius Cicero {#statesman-28a}

- 1 Free Tribune per turn.
- +2 to Trial dice roll if Prosecutor or Advocate.

## 29A — M. Licinius Crassus {#statesman-29a}

- Doubles base Senatorial and Concession income but not Knight income.
- May be played even if 29B is already in play.

## 29B — L. Licinius Lucullus {#statesman-29b}

- Nullifies any losses taken as a result of a Disaster/Standoff. The War is considered Unprosecuted and Lucullus will automatically be recalled to Rome immediately after the next Senate Phase closes.
- May be played even if 29A is already in play.

## 30A — CN. Pompeius Magnus {#statesman-30a}

- Doubles Popularity Gains / Losses.
- MIL determined before each battle.
- MIL is 2 when considering Minimum Force Requirements.
