## 3.04 EXTENDED SCENARIO {#3.04}

### 3.04.1 EXTENDED SCENARIO {#3.04.1}

Rather than simply playing one scenario, continue into the Middle (or Late) Republic by shuffling the second (or third) deck from that period and placing it in play. Players may play just two decks (Early to Middle or Middle to Late) or opt for the complete extended Game (Early to Middle to Late). Play proceeds normally after noting the increased Legion Loyalty numbers for Revolts ([1.11.31](../01-basic-game/1.11-revolution-phase.md#1.11.31)) and the new Random Events Table column in use for the new period. Only in the Extended Scenario, the Game is considered to be in the Middle or Late period the moment a card is drawn from that deck (the drawing player must announce he drew from the new period). Setup should be done for the first deck only according to the starting Scenario for that deck, except that the placement of the "Era Ends" card should be done by using the Era Ends placement rule for the ending Scenario deck.

> **EXAMPLE:** If doing the Extended Scenario from the Middle to Late scenarios, follow all the steps in the Middle Republic Scenario ([3.02](../03-scenarios/3.02-middle-republic.md#3.02)) except for steps (3.02.2 G and 3.02.2 H). Instead, use step (3.03.2 H) from the Late Republic Scenario ([3.03](../03-scenarios/3.03-late-republic.md#3.03)) to place the Era Ends card.
