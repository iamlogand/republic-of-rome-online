## 3.05 ALTERNATIVE HISTORY SCENARIO {#3.05}

### 3.05.1 ALTERNATE HISTORY SCENARIO {#3.05.1}

Set up the Game exactly as the Early Republic Scenario ([3.01](../03-scenarios/3.01-early-republic.md#3.01)), except ignore all deck borders [e.g., deal out 3 random black-text Family cards (from any scenario) and 3 random Faction cards (from any scenario)]. The deck will consist of all 3 decks shuffled together. The Random Events column and Legion Loyalty Numbers will advance at the start of the 8th and 16th turns. Randomly shuffle the "Era Ends" card into the last 12 cards of the combined deck.
