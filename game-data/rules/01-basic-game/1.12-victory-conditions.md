# 1.12. Victory Conditions {#1.12}

[← I. Basic Game](README.md) | [Rules Index](../README.md)

Each Losing Condition takes precedence over all Winning Conditions.

### 1.12.1 LOSING CONDITIONS {#1.12.1}

The Game ends in defeat for **all** players when one of the following Losing Conditions occurs:

1. There are 4 or more Active Wars at the end of any Combat Phase. A Civil War is an Active War unless the Primary Rebel has met one of his Winning Conditions (1 or 2 from [1.12.2](#1.12.2)).
2. A result of "People Revolt" is obtained during a Population Phase ([1.08.2](../01-basic-game/1.08-population-phase.md#1.08.2)) with no Senator in Revolt.
3. The State Treasury goes Bankrupt (i.e., the State is unable to pay for something when required) ([1.06.53](../01-basic-game/1.06-revenue-phase.md#1.06.53), [1.07.21](../01-basic-game/1.07-forum-phase.md#1.07.21)) with no Senator in Revolt.

### 1.12.2 WINNING CONDITIONS {#1.12.2}

The Game ends with a win for a player controlling Senator(s) that fulfil any of the following Winning Conditions, listed in order from highest to lowest precedence:

1. A Senator in Revolt, while Marching on Rome, defeats the Senate in battle ([1.11.373](../01-basic-game/1.11-revolution-phase.md#1.11.373)) without dying in that battle ([1.10.7](../01-basic-game/1.10-combat-phase.md#1.10.7)), or the Senate fails to attack his Army in a Combat Phase ([1.11.36](../01-basic-game/1.11-revolution-phase.md#1.11.36)). Proceed to the Rebel End Game ([1.12.3](#1.12.3)).
2. The State Treasury goes Bankrupt ([1.06.53](../01-basic-game/1.06-revenue-phase.md#1.06.53), [1.07.21](../01-basic-game/1.07-forum-phase.md#1.07.21)) or the People Revolt ([1.08.2](../01-basic-game/1.08-population-phase.md#1.08.2)) while a Senator is in Revolt. Proceed to the Rebel End Game ([1.12.3](#1.12.3)).
3. A **Forum** Phase ends in which the Era Ends card was revealed. If a Consul for Life ([1.09.82](../01-basic-game/1.09-senate-phase.md#1.09.82)) is alive at the end of such a Forum Phase, he wins (even if he is held Captive). If there is no Consul for Life at the end of such a Forum Phase, the Faction with the most combined Influence from non-rebel, non-Captive Senators wins. If combined Faction Influence is tied, break the tie in favor of the non-rebel, non-Captive Senator with the highest individual Influence in the tied Factions. If still tied, the tied player with the highest current total of votes (not counting Talents) wins.
4. A Senator is elected ([1.09.821](../01-basic-game/1.09-senate-phase.md#1.09.821)) or appointed ([1.09.822](../01-basic-game/1.09-senate-phase.md#1.09.822)) Consul for Life ([1.09.823](../01-basic-game/1.09-senate-phase.md#1.09.823)) and survives to the end of a Revolution Phase (and is still Consul for Life then). A Consul for Life may win while held Captive ([1.10.71](../01-basic-game/1.10-combat-phase.md#1.10.71)).

### 1.12.3 REBEL END GAME {#1.12.3}

If there are fewer than 4 Active Wars immediately after a Rebel achieves either of his Winning Conditions (1 or 2 from [1.12.2](#1.12.2)), he wins. If instead there are 4 or more Active Wars, skip directly to the Combat Phase (if not already in that Phase). All surviving Legions except Garrisons return to the Active Forces boxes. The Primary Rebel then takes all Legions in the Active Forces boxes and all remaining non-Provincial Fleets (wherever they are) as his Force. He then attacks Active Wars one at a time, in the order of his choosing. He must have sufficient Fleet Support and gain a Victory and avoid being killed in each previous battle in order to attack each War.

If the Rebel is unable to reduce the number of Active Wars to fewer than 4, all players lose. If he reduces the number of Active Wars to fewer than 4, he wins, even if he dies in the last necessary battle. \[EXCEPTION: If the Rebel gains a Victory but dies in the last battle necessary to reduce the Active Wars to fewer than 4, and his Winning Condition was 1 from [1.12.2](#1.12.2), the Rebel does not win. In that case, if a Consul for Life is alive, he wins, or if there is no Consul for Life, the Revolt fails ([1.11.372](../01-basic-game/1.11-revolution-phase.md#1.11.372)) and the game continues.\]
