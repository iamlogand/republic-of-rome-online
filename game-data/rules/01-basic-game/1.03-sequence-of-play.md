## 1.03 SEQUENCE OF PLAY {#1.03}

Each turn is modeled on the political year of the Roman Republic wherein lackluster "years" are skipped to keep the action constant. Each turn is composed of seven Phases.

1. MORTALITY PHASE ([1.05](../01-basic-game/1.05-mortality-phase.md#1.05))
2. REVENUE PHASE ([1.06](../01-basic-game/1.06-revenue-phase.md#1.06))
3. FORUM PHASE ([1.07](../01-basic-game/1.07-forum-phase.md#1.07))
4. POPULATION PHASE ([1.08](../01-basic-game/1.08-population-phase.md#1.08))
5. SENATE PHASE ([1.09](../01-basic-game/1.09-senate-phase.md#1.09))
6. COMBAT PHASE ([1.10](../01-basic-game/1.10-combat-phase.md#1.10))
7. REVOLUTION PHASE ([1.11](../01-basic-game/1.11-revolution-phase.md#1.11))
